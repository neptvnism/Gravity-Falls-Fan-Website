Dipper Pines

A curious and adventurous boy who is always eager to solve mysteries.

Dipper is often seen with a journal that contains information on the mysteries of Gravity Falls. He is intelligent and determined, though sometimes overthinks situations.

Mabel Pines

Dipper’s twin sister who is fun-loving and optimistic.

Read More
Mabel is known for her colorful sweaters and bubbly personality. She enjoys arts and crafts, and her positive outlook often helps the group out of tricky situations.
"Trust no one, kid."

Grunkle Stan

The great-uncle of Dipper and Mabel who runs the Mystery Shack.

Read More
Grunkle Stan is a shrewd and cynical con artist, but he cares deeply for Dipper and Mabel. He has many secrets, and his past is a key part of the show’s mysteries.
"Dude, am I right? Or am I right?"

Soos Ramirez

The handyman at the Mystery Shack who is loyal and good-natured.

Read More
Soos is like an older brother figure to Dipper and Mabel. He’s always eager to help out, even if he’s a bit clumsy. His optimism is contagious, and he’s a fan favorite.
